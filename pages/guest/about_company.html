About Revaux

Last updated on 07/25/2025

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

I. Introduction

Welcome to Revaux, a local clothing brand from the Philippines committed to reinventing modern fashion. The name Revaux blends two powerful ideas: Re symbolizes Reinvent, Refresh, and Retro, while Vaux embodies a high-fashion essence. We merge streetwear, Y2K, old money, and minimalist styles to create fashion that’s both timeless and contemporary.

II. Our Vision

At Revaux, we believe that fashion should never compromise quality or accessibility. We are passionate about creating high-quality products that are affordable and accessible for Filipinos, offering an option to elevate their style without breaking the bank. Our goal is to bring local craftsmanship to a global level while making sure our designs remain within reach of the average Filipino shopper.

III. Product Categories

Revaux offers a versatile selection of clothing and accessories, carefully curated to fit the needs of our modern customers. Our collections are segmented into four main categories:

  • Headwear: Caps, Eyewear, Hats, and Bandanas
  • Top: T-shirts, Polos, Sweaters, and Hoodies
  • Bottom: Jeans, Shorts, Trousers, and Cargo Pants
  • Footwear: Sneakers, Sandals, Boots, and Loafers

IV. High-Quality, Affordable Fashion

We’re driven by a desire to offer our customers the perfect balance of premium quality and affordability. Each piece in our collection is made with precision, care, and the finest materials, ensuring longevity and comfort. We aim to provide the quality you would expect from high-end brands while making sure our prices remain within reach for every Filipino. At Revaux, everyone deserves to look and feel their best without compromising on their budget.

V. Our Philosophy

Fashion should be an expression of your individuality without requiring you to spend a fortune. That’s why Revaux focuses on offering designs that are both high in quality and affordable. We believe in reinvention—taking inspiration from the past and fusing it with modern trends to create something timeless. Our designs speak to the sophisticated, stylish Filipino who knows the value of quality at the right price.

VI. Expanding Boundaries

At Revaux, we’re not just about clothing. We’re about creating a community, where fashion meets affordability and inclusivity. As we grow, we aspire to not only dominate the local market but to expand globally. Our mission is to make high-fashion designs accessible to all, proving that style and quality can be within everyone's reach.

VII. Join the Revolution

Be part of the Revaux revolution—a movement where reinvention meets high fashion, without compromising your budget. Whether you’re shopping for headwear, tops, bottoms, or footwear, Revaux promises a shopping experience that is as stylish as it is affordable. We invite you to explore our collections and find pieces that fit both your style and your budget.
